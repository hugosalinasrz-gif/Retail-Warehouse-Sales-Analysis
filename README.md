## 1. Project Overview
This report presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the performance of retail and warehouse sales. The main goal is to visualize sales distribution, identify key product trends, and compare retail versus warehouse performance to uncover actionable business insights.

## 3. Strategic Insights (Teaser)
The full analysis (available in the PDF report) goes beyond simple KPIs to identify critical risks and structural opportunities for the business.

* **Insight 1:** Category Concentration Risk & SKU Inefficiency
* **Insight 2:** Seasonal Cash Flow & Anomaly Detection
* **Insight 3:** Discovery of Three Distinct Business Models
* **Insight 4:** Supplier Base Defines Operating Model
